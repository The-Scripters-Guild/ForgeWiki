# Invasion

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/cover-tsg-placeholder.jpg" alt="Cover image"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Invasion is an asymmetrical, one-sided objective mode that pits two teams of eight against each other. The mode provides a tactical experience where one team attacks and the other defends, utilizing specialized loadouts that change as the match progresses through different phases.

## Match Structure and Scoring

Matches consist of two rounds, with each team having the opportunity to play as both the attacker and the defender. Each round is divided into three distinct phases.

### Objective Types

The objectives available during each phase are based on modes found in *Halo: Reach*, with the addition of a new objective type. The possible objectives include:

* Capture the Zone
* Assault
* Core Delivery
* Deliver the Payload

### Victory Conditions

Teams earn points by successfully completing each phase. Completing all three phases in a round grants the team three points. If a defending team successfully stops the attackers during a specific phase, the attacking team only earns points for the phases they completed prior to being stopped. It is possible for a match to result in a tie if both teams successfully complete all three phases and earn three points each.

## Spawning Mechanics

Invasion includes a "Buddy Spawning" feature, which allows players to spawn directly on a randomly assigned teammate rather than at a default spawn location. Buddies are assigned at the start of the match and remain paired for the duration of the game.

{% hint style="warning" %}
If a buddy leaves the match, the ability to use Buddy Spawning is no longer available for that player.
{% endhint %}

### Buddy Spawning Requirements

To utilize Buddy Spawning, several conditions must be met:

* The relationship is reciprocal; if a player is a buddy to someone, that person is also their buddy.
* The buddy must be currently alive.
* The buddy must have been alive for at least five seconds.
* The buddy must have full health and shields.
* The buddy must be clear of engagements or have no nearby enemies.
* If the buddy is in a vehicle, there must be an available seat.
* Upon spawning, the player will face the same direction as their buddy.

Players who do not wish to use Buddy Spawning can select to spawn at the Left, Middle, or Right spawn zones.
